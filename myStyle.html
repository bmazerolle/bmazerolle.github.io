myStyle: A Neural Network Based Outfit Generation App

As a busy, often rushed, college student, I found myself annoyed with the task of choosing what to wear every morning. After some thought, I realized that the power of a simple Meural Network would take the thinking out of the process for me. Thus, myStyle was born.

myStyle is designed to be an iOS and Android app that generates 5 unique outfits per day for users. They are all selected using a Neural Network that considers factors such as the weather, what's clean, compatible colors, and what users have worn in the past.

The app's UI has been completed for our school project (we received 98%), and we are now working on the development of the neural networks that will be used in outfit generation and photo recognition for wardrobe uploads.
